Admissions

Admission Guidelines

Admissions are open for the upcoming academic year. We welcome students from all backgrounds.

Process

  1. Collect admission form from the school office.
  2. Submit required documents: birth certificate, previous school records, photographs.
  3. Attend interaction or interview if required.
  4. Receive confirmation and pay fees.
