Admission Process

  1. Online Registration: Fill out the admission form available on our website.
  2. Document Submission: Submit required documents as per the guidelines.
  3. Interaction/Assessment: Attend the scheduled interaction/assessment session.
  4. Admission Confirmation: Receive admission confirmation and complete formalities.

Eligibility Criteria

Admissions are open for the following classes:

  • Nursery: Children born between April 1, 2019, and March 31, 2020.
  • Kindergarten: Children born between April 1, 2018, and March 31, 2019.
  • Class I: Children born between April 1, 2017, and March 31, 2018.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Residence Proof
  • Parent's ID Proof
  • Passport-sized Photographs
