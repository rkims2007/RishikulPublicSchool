Admissions

Admission Process

We welcome students from Class KG to Class 10. Admissions are based on merit, interview, and document verification.

  • Step 1: Fill the admission inquiry form.
  • Step 2: Attend the interaction session with teachers and principal.
  • Step 3: Submit required documents.
  • Step 4: Confirmation of admission.

For more information, please contact our admissions office.
